About


Most floods take hours or even days to develop, giving residents ample time to prepare or evacuate. Others generate quickly and with little warning. These flash floods can be extremely dangerous, instantly turning a babbling brook into a thundering wall of water and sweeping everything in its path downstream.
Flood depends on the complex mixture of rainfall, soil moisture and the recent history of precipitation. Snowmelts and storm surges can also contribute to unexpected floods.
We are the Dark Phoenix team of BIT Mesra. Our website provides you with all the details you need to know about flooding in your area. The website has a very friendly user interface. The site contains various info box which will guide you through the website. The map shows major flood zones in India with red circles. The degree of redness indicates the degree of deaths. Due to lack of data we will use central water commission to provide results of prediction. The website shows warnings and will also send emails to subscribers and respective authorities of the locality.
You can subscribe to our website using your email address to receive regular updates about flood forecast.
Since social media is the largest source of audience, we also have our facebook page "Let's cope with disaster" which gives warnings. Make sure to like the page.
